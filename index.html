About Me

Hi! I'm an experienced mechanical engineer with a demonstrated history of innovation across numerous fields including robotics, consumer electronics, medical product development and more. Skilled in mechanical design, problem dissection, and creative thinking. Excited by creating and implementing novel solutions to challenging problems.
